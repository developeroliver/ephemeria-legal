Welcome to HabitDaily. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our app. We are committed to safeguarding your data and ensuring transparency about how your information is handled.

Data Storage & Sync via iCloud

HabitDaily uses Apple's iCloud and SwiftData to store and sync your habit tracking data across your devices.

What We Store:

  • Habit data: Habit names, icons, colors, categories, frequencies, notes
  • Check-in data: Completion dates, skip reasons, status (completed/skipped/missed)
  • Reminders: Notification preferences, custom messages, notification times
  • App settings: Theme preferences, color palettes, onboarding status

How It's Stored:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData
  • Data is automatically synced to your personal iCloud account if you have iCloud enabled
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data - it's encrypted and stored in your Apple account
  • You maintain full control over your data through your iCloud settings

Subscription & Purchase Management via StoreKit

HabitDaily uses Apple's StoreKit 2 to provide premium subscriptions, ensuring a secure and privacy-focused experience.

What Apple Collects:

  • Purchase transaction details (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Subscription status and renewal information
  • All payment information is processed by Apple - we never see your credit card details

What We Store Locally:

  • Premium status: A simple boolean flag (isPremium: true/false)
  • Purchase date: The date you became a premium member
  • No personal identifiers: We don't collect or store any personal information related to purchases

How Subscriptions Work:

  • All subscription management happens through Apple's App Store
  • Purchases are validated directly with Apple's servers
  • We use StoreKit 2's built-in security features to verify subscriptions
  • No third-party payment processors are used

Your Control:
